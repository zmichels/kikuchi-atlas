## Scientific scope

This is a kinematical Ice Ih proof at 20 keV, not a dynamical EBSD ground
truth. It uses the fully occupied oxygen site from
[COD 1572233](https://www.crystallography.net/cod/1572233.html), refined from
electron-diffraction data at 81.15 K in `P 63/m m c` with
`a = b = 4.3815 A` and `c = 7.183 A`. The tracked derivative deliberately
omits the two half-occupied hydrogen sites and names itself
`ice-Ih-oxygen-sublattice`; it must not be presented as a complete ordered-H
model.

The original COD CIF checksum remains recorded alongside the checksum of the
minimal redistributable derivative. The `[001]` orientation, identity source
setting, detector geometry, reflection thresholds, tone map, and quiet overlay
are all recipe-controlled.

## Validated evidence

- The derived CIF verifies as oxygen-only, space group 194, with one
  asymmetric O site at `(1/3, 2/3, 0.4378)` and multiplicity four.
- The adapter supports explicit identity and cyclic unsigned axis
  permutations and uses the full hexagonal lattice metric for direction
  checks.
- `diffsims` does not implement its `allowed` shortcut for primitive
  hexagonal lattices. The bounded fallback correctly treats a primitive cell
  as having no centering extinctions, expands the unit cell, and relies on
  calculated structure factors for screw/glide extinctions. The forbidden
  `001` reflection is tested as absent from the retained catalog.
- The focused Ice and forsterite regression suites pass: 35 tests total.
- The full run retains 968 master reflectors and emits six figures plus plain
  array, catalog, recipe, source, and projection-ledger evidence.
- Quiet-image SHA-256:
  `28d6f340755f6c6a7c4517b76ae78f79684e9810473080daef69af9512123fc5`.

## Visual gate

The quiet circular master is the review candidate. It preserves the promoted
forsterite treatment while exposing the strong sixfold geometry of `[001]`
Ice Ih. No blur or post-simulation smoothing is introduced.

The detector overlay is retained as a diagnostic, not promoted as an art
product: at the present threshold it is too annotation-dense. Improving that
view should reduce or rank vector overlays, not blur the simulated intensity.

## Non-claims and next decisions

- This one second-phase proof does not establish arbitrary-CIF or universal
  phase support.
- Hydrogen disorder, temperature-dependent lattice variants, dynamical
  scattering, and experimental pattern matching remain outside this proof.
- A room-temperature/ambient-cell variant near the commonly quoted
  `a approximately 4.52 A`, `c approximately 7.36 A` can be added later as a
  separately sourced recipe rather than silently replacing this refinement.
- Orientation variation should be generated by an explicit spherical
  rotation/reprojection contract; changing detector orientation alone does
  not create a differently oriented crystal-frame master plot.
